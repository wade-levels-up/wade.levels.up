About Me

I'm a 33yo living in the heart of Melbourne Australia. In my spare time I love learning to code, playing video games, spending time with my partner and enjoying a coffee (or two).

Read more

For me, life is about pursuing the heights of my creativity, It's about spending my time doing the things I love with the people I love. I'm a detail-focused person who is also intense, disciplined and driven - but also just a big kid once you get to know me.

Whilst naturally quite introverted, investing time into interpersonal skills has helped me develop an ability to network and connect that I can turn on when required.

Starting late March of 2024 I began teaching myself web development where I'm now learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Git, Express and React. Knowing how to code has been an itch I've wanted to scratch since I was in my early teens playing around with a program called Game Maker. Making games has been a lifelong dream of mine, but I didn't know how to code that young - nor did I have the patience or anyone to learn from. Returning to coding at a more mature age with life experience and having achieved other milestones has meant I've finally been able to make ground on what I couldn't when I was young - It's an exciting time for me.

There's a lot to learn, but I feel I've never been in a better position to pursue it. I'm excited to see where this journey leads!
